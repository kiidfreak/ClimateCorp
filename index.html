Our Action Areas

Carbon Corp aims to Rewarding Green Efforts with Blockchain Technology to Scale Carbon Markets.

Tracking

Sustainable activities are tracked using IoT devices and smart meters, with data securely logged on the blockchain.

Rewards

Companies can earn and trade Carbon Corps tokens for engaging in sustainable practices.

MarketPlace

Companies buy tokens to offset emissions; these tokens are burned to create CC Certificates as proof of their green efforts.

Carbon Corp's mission is to use blockchain technology to track sustainable activities and reward companies with tradeable tokens for their green effort promoting socio-economic benefits for communities globally.
